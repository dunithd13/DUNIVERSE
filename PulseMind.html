PulseMind is a microcontroller based automated healthcare monitoring system through a wearable device on a wrist which focused on measuring both mental and physical health. It monitors body temperature, heart rate and stress index in real time using ESP32 microcontroller and a combination of sensors including DS18B20 temperature sensor and Heart Rate sensor.
All data is transmitted to MySQL database through wifi and displayed on Health professional web dashboard.

This is a group project and I am very lucky to lead this amazing group including Thisen Nambukara, Tharidi Gamage, Faizam Fairooz, Nehansa Denipiyage and Yasindu Sasmitha.
